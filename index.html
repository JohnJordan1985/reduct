reduct verb (Philosophy)

To trace (a thing) back to its origin.

If “abstraction” is the process of going from the things we can directly observe—the trees—to the concepts that we can only imagine—the forest—then “reduction” is the reverse process.

To reduct a concept is to trace that concept back, step-by-step, to its originating source in the things that we can directly experience.
